About Gary

Your Local Vermont Neighbor

Greetings! This is Gary Stuard, the Pie Guy and Welcome to my website. I have 30+ years of baking experience, having been inspired by my mother's and her mother's Grandma Cregh, exceptional baking skills. I have won awards for my pies (i.e my lavender blueberry pie and salted caramel apple pie.) I have also recently begun teaching pie making classes online through City Market in Burlington, VT, having now taught viewers how to make my Dark Rum Exta Ginger Pumpkin Pie, Maple Coffee Chocolate Walnut Pie, and Macadamia Data Pie. I make all kinds of pies and tarts: fruite, nut, cream and chocolate based, as well as steamed puddings cheesecakes, and rustic fruite based cakes. You name it! Only the best organic ingredients and everything is made by scratch.
